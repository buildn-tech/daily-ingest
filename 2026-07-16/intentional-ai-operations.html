productupdated 2026-06-25

ChatGPT Finances preview

What changed
U.S. Plus/Pro users can connect accounts via Plaid, see a consolidated dashboard, and use dedicated financial memories for goals and context.
Buildn / client use
The transferable lesson for client work is consented context: connected data becomes useful only with narrow purpose, reviewable outputs, and clear opt-out.

Caveat: U.S.-only rollout; financial data is sensitive and the product is not financial advice. Do not connect business/client accounts casually.

Next move: Read the data controls first; if eligible, test with a low-risk personal use case and document the consent boundary.
