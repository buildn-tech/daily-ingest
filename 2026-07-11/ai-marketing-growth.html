Dynamic Search Ads transition to AI Max delayed to February 2027

turn into taskproduct/updateJun 11
  • Source: Google Blog

What it is

Google extended the DSA sunset/auto-upgrade timeline to February 2027 while ACA and campaign-level broad match upgrades still start September 2026.

Why it matters

This gives advertisers breathing room to test AI Max, update landing pages, and set controls before legacy search automation changes.

Useful now

The June update changes migration timing; teams relying on old DSA need a calendar and test plan.

Use cases for Abdelrahman / Buildn

  • Client migration checklist
  • Campaign audit service
  • Landing-page readiness
  • Search controls review

Caveats

AI Max performance claims are averages; run holdouts where possible.

Follow-up

Create a DSA-to-AI-Max audit task template.
