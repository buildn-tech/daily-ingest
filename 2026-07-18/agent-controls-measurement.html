What: daily API data for coding-agent PRs, reviews, and suggestion types per repo.
Buildn/client use: compare adoption, merges, and review load by project.
Caveat: activity is not quality.

Buildn/client use: identify exploration versus engineering execution.
Caveat: fields are null without app activity.

Buildn/client use: clear low-risk review queues while away from desk.
Caveat: retain merge approval.
