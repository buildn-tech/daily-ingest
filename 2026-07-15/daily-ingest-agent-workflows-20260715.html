Why nowTool-heavy workflows can become cheaper and less brittle when orchestration happens near the tools.
Buildn / client useUse it for research aggregation, audit evidence collection, or multi-source reporting with a small final evidence set.
Caveat: Model/vendor performance claims are not a substitute for a representative Buildn benchmark.

GPT-Live

product2026-07-08
  • DIRECT · https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-live/
What it isA full-duplex voice system that keeps conversation flowing while delegating search, reasoning and agent work in the background.
Why nowVoice becomes a control plane for field notes, meeting capture and quick task delegation—not only a dictation layer.
Buildn / client useTry spoken discovery interviews or on-the-go project capture, then require written review before tasks are created.
Caveat: API access is not yet generally available; do not design production workflows around launch UI alone.
