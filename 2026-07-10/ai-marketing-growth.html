What it is
HubSpot's marketplace and studio for marketing, sales, and service agents including prospecting, customer, data, health, research, and closing agents.
Why it matters
It shows CRM agents maturing into configurable teammates that work inside approved business data rather than standalone chatbots.
Use cases for Abdelrahman / Buildn

Useful now

Watch-out: Agent output quality follows CRM hygiene; dirty records create confident nonsense. Follow-up: save.
